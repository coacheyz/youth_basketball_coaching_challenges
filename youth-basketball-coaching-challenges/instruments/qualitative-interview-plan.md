# Qualitative Data Plan — Youth Basketball Coaching Challenges

**Appendix C — Interview Protocol**

---

## Selection Process

Coaches selected for interviews represent the typical coach in the research group. Selection criteria:

- Must have at least one current child participating in a basketball program
- Must be at least eighteen years old
- Must have expressed interest in the survey and want to be informed on future results
- Must have experience participating in youth basketball at some point in their adolescence

From this criteria, **five coaches** were selected to be interviewed for approximately one hour each, at a neutral location (food or beverage setting) at the researcher's expense.

---

## Interview Questions

### Background & Socialization

**1. What was your experience being involved in youth basketball growing up?**
- Positive or negative?
- How did this influence the way you coach?

---

### Coaching Challenges

**2. What is the biggest challenge to coaching youth basketball?**

For each of the following factors, ask: *Please expand on how this can be improved.*

- Time Commitment
- Referees
- Sports organization
- Parents
- Players
- Own child(ren)
- Home/Life
- Spouse
- Job/Coach balance
- Family commitment
- Other

---

### Memorable Experiences

**3. What has been your most memorable coaching experience?**

- *Positive:* Please elaborate on how this experience has influenced your decision to coach in the proceeding seasons.
- *Negative:* Please elaborate on how this will influence your decision to coach in the proceeding season.

---

### Resources & Motivation

**4. What is the most helpful resource to your coaching experience?**
- How has this influenced your willingness to coach in the future?
- Is this a resource that can be utilized every season?

---

### Defining Success and Failure

**5. How do you define success in coaching youth basketball?**

**6. How do you define failure in coaching youth basketball?**

---

### Mentorship & Role

**7. In what way do you consider yourself to be a mentor to the players you coach?**

**8. Describe the perfect coaching situation for you to be successful.**

---

### Pressure & Conflict

**9. Do you experience pressure from any one person when coaching?**
- Who?
- How do you interpret that pressure?
  - *Positive:* Can you explain how it improves your ability to coach?
  - *Negative:* Can you explain how it inhibits your ability to coach?

---

### Change & Growth

**10. If you could change one aspect of the coaching experience, what would that be?**

**11. How do you deal with conflict with a parent of one of the players on your team who disagrees with your coaching style?**
- Adapt from criticism
- Neglect criticism

**12. How have you changed as a coach from when you first began coaching?**

---

### Advice

**13. What advice would you give a new youth basketball coach to better prepare for the season?**

---

### Closing

**14. What is the biggest challenge to coaching youth basketball?**
*(Asked again at the end to compare with the response given at the beginning of the interview)*

---

*Interviews were approximately one hour in length and conducted at a neutral public location.*
