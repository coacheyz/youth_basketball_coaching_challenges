**15. Unseen player reaction**

**16. Overbearing parent**
A parent of one of your lesser talented players is outraged with the amount of playing time that their child has received. Because of their concerns, you have decided to give this player more minutes in order to avoid future confrontations with the family.

`Strongly Disagree` — `Disagree` — `Neutral` — `Agree` — `Strongly Agree`

---

**17. Selfish player**
During a timeout, you draw up a play for player #32 to come off two screens in order to get an open shot. One of the players that you have instructed to set the screen has exclaimed that he wants to be the player designated to shoot. You adjust the play to have him shooting instead of #32.

`Strongly Disagree` — `Disagree` — `Neutral` — `Agree` — `Strongly Agree`

---

**18. Center/PG conflict**
Player #1 is your most talented center position player, as he has really adapted his playstyle to mesh perfectly with your coaching style. His parents are concerned that you are not utilizing him correctly as they feel he should be a point guard and have voiced their issues with you. As a result, you have now made Player #1 your starting point guard for the next game to please his parents.

`Strongly Disagree` — `Disagree` — `Neutral` — `Agree` — `Strongly Agree`

**19. Bench sub — crowd pressure**
2 minutes left in the final game of your season. The crowd/fans have begun chanting the name of one of the players on the bench in order to influence you to play this player. Due to the crowd pressure, you have succumbed and told the player to check into the game.

**20. Free throw sub — player pressure**
